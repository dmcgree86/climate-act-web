Why This Matters Now

From 1 January 2025, Australian companies face new climate‑related financial disclosure obligations under the Treasury Laws Amendment (Financial Market Infrastructure and Other Measures) Act 2024. While many providers focus on emissions and transition risk, the legislation explicitly requires disclosure of material physical climate risks — multi‑peril in nature and evolving as the climate warms. Few organisations can quantify these risks, integrate them into financial reporting, and present them in a form that meets regulatory and strategic needs.
